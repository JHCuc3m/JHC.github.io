Jiahao Chen

M.S. in Computational Science and Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology

I'm a Master's student in Computational Science and Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I previously completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Carlos III University of Madrid, which included exchange studies at the University of Waterloo and Georgia Tech.

My research is at the intersection of machine learning and systems, focusing on how ML can accelerate symbolic reasoning tools like SMT solvers, and how compiler technology can accelerate ML models on existing infrastructure.

Outside of academics, I enjoy going to the gym and playing basketball. I was part of the Ultimate Frisbee team that won 1st place in the Spanish National Div. 2 in 2024, and I recently joined the Georgia Tech competitive ballroom team.
